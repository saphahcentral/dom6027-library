Faith and Health: Living in Covenant Wellness

Spiritual health influences physical health. Scripture encourages believers to honor their bodies as YHVH’s temple. Observing Torah dietary laws, keeping the Shabbat, and trusting in YHVH’s provision all contribute to holistic well-being. Faith strengthens the immune system through peace, gratitude, and joy.

Steps for Covenant Wellness

  • Follow clean and wholesome eating guidelines
  • Rest according to Torah rhythms, including Shabbat
  • Practice daily prayer and gratitude
  • Engage in physical activity within creation’s bounds
