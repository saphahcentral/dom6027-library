The Return of Yahusha: Hope for the Faithful

Yahusha’s return is the central hope of covenant people. Scripture outlines clear indicators, prophetic fulfillment, and the call for readiness. Believers are encouraged to walk in obedience, prayer, and watchfulness, aligning with Torah and covenant principles.

Preparation Guidelines

  • Strengthen knowledge of Torah and Prophets
  • Maintain covenantal holiness in daily life
  • Encourage and build up the covenant community
  • Recognize and avoid spiritual deception
