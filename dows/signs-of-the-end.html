Signs of the End Times: Observing the Times Biblically

The Torah and the Prophets give clear signs that precede YHVH’s final intervention in history. Observing the cycles, societal moral decline, and upheavals in creation can help believers discern the times. The Bible warns against deception but encourages watchfulness.

Key Observations

  • Increase in false teachings and corruption of Torah principles
  • Wars, natural disasters, and social upheavals that echo prophecy
  • Global alliances and powers shifting in ways Scripture describes
  • Signs in the heavens — celestial indicators tied to appointed times
