From Babel to Babylon: Lessons in Human Pride and Dispersion

Humanity’s rebellion at Babel foreshadows the rise of Babylon as a symbol of pride, conquest, and oppression. Scripture traces the consequences of arrogance and the scattering of people. Observing these events provides insight into patterns that repeat in the last days.

Key Takeaways

  • Pride leads to confusion and separation from God
  • God preserves a remnant to fulfill covenant promises
  • Understanding history aids in recognizing end-time parallels
