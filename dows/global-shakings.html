Global Shakings: Understanding Natural and Societal Upheavals

Scripture portrays periods of shaking — both in the earth and among nations. Observing these events helps the faithful prepare and discern the times. It is a call to stability in covenant obedience despite external turbulence.

Patterns to Note

  • Earthquakes, storms, and environmental anomalies
  • Rapid political change or collapse of governments
  • Social unrest indicating moral decay
