Building a Ministry Website: Practical Training for Beginners

A ministry website should be clear, secure, and mission-focused. It does not need to be complex to be effective. Start with mission clarity, simple navigation, and reliable hosting.

Core Pages to Include

  • Home – clear mission statement and calls to action
  • Resources – sermon notes, articles, downloadable guides (Paleo-Hebrew PDFs)
  • Contact – secure form and clear response times
  • Support – donation info and transparency policies

Security & Maintenance

Keep software up to date, enable backups, and use strong authentication. Plan a simple content calendar and appoint someone to maintain uploads and corrections.
