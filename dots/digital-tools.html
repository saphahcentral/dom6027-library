Digital Tools for Torah Study: Using Technology Wisely

Technology can multiply access to Torah resources, but it must remain a servant — not a substitute — for discipleship. Choose tools that increase understanding, preserve integrity, and encourage community application.

Recommended Tool Types

  • Interlinear and lexicon apps for language work
  • Audio recordings for pronunciation and prayer practice
  • Note-taking and reference managers for research
  • Private community platforms for secure teaching and feedback

Best Practices

Verify sources, avoid over-reliance on a single tool, and combine digital work with communal reading and discussion. Backup data regularly and protect access to ministry accounts.
