Messianic Leadership: Principles for Training and Discipleship

Leadership in Derech Olam arises from servanthood and fidelity to Torah. A Messianic leader models Messiah: humility, teaching, sacrifice, and shepherding. True leadership trains others to serve, protects the flock, and preserves covenant truth.

Five Foundational Principles

  • Servanthood: Leading by example, not by title or coercion.
  • Teaching: Prioritize Scripture, language, and the Mo'edim in training.
  • Accountability: Transparent leadership subject to community correction.
  • Discipleship: Train leaders who make leaders — a culture of multiplication.
  • Spiritual Formation: Encourage prayer, fasting, and personal obedience.

Practical Training Models

Use short apprenticeships, mentoring, and hands-on service rotations. Assess character as much as competence. Create brief modules: Torah foundations, pastoral care, worship, digital ministry, and community logistics.
