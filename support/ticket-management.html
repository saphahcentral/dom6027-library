Ticket Management: Efficient Workflow for Support

Proper ticket management ensures that every user request is logged, tracked, and resolved in an organized manner. This system helps prevent lost requests, overlapping efforts, and delays in resolution.

Best Practices for Ticket Management

  • Assign tickets based on skill and availability
  • Set realistic response and resolution times
  • Maintain a clear history of each issue
  • Regularly review and close resolved tickets
